Only Two Fundamental Limitations

1. Long-term Context Management

  • No true memory across sessions

2. Online Learning

  • Can't learn from experience — every session is a blank slate

Everything else is tractable engineering — and it more than compensates.

The key point: even without solving these, the agent and orchestration layers compensate. The next slides show how much is possible already.
